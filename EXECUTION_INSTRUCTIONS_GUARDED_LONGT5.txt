Execution Instructions for Guarded LongT5 Summarizer

1. Open the live Hugging Face Space: https://huggingface.co/spaces/harshitmahour360/LonGT5Summarizer
2. Upload a legal/document PDF in the app interface.
3. Click “Extract PDF Text” to convert the PDF into cleaned text.
4. Keep Mode as “Balanced” for normal testing.
5. Keep “Use extractive factual skeleton” enabled for better factual summaries.
6. Keep Anchor Method as “None” unless deeper testing is required.
7. Click “Generate Guarded Summary” to produce the final summary.
8. Check the Final Summary, Section Summaries, and Debug logs for output quality.
